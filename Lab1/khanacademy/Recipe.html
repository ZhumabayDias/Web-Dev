Recipe #1

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Cook Time: 2 hours, 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 2 hours, 45 minutes
  • Serves: 3 to 4
Ingredients Quantity
teaspoons olive oil 3
pound beef stew meat 1
cup chopped onion 1

Step 1:Heat 2 teaspoons of oil in a large sauté pan or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Brown the meat on one side for 5 minutes without turning over. Sprinkle with salt & pepper.

Step 2:Turn meat, add 1 tsp. oil & brown another 2-3 minutes. Sprinkle with salt & pepper. Do not crowd the meat. Brown in two batches if necessary.

Step 3: With all the meat browned and in the pan, reduce heat to medium. Add onion & garlic. Cook and stir 2-3 minutes until onion is browned.

Step 4: Add stock, salt, thyme & bay leaf. Meat should be submerged so add more stock if needed to cover the meat.

Step 5: Bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat & simmer for 2 hours, checking occasionally.

Step 6: After 2 hours, stir in potatoes, carrots & celery and bring to a boil. Cover & simmer 10-15 minutes until vegetables are done.

Step 7:Combine flour with 1/4 cup stock or water, whisking until there are no lumps. Stir into stew and cook 5 more minutes until thickened.

Recipe #2

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 5 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
cup finely diced onion 3/4
cup finely diced red bell pepper 1/4
Tablespoon chopped fresh parsley 1

Step 1:Place drained salmon in a bowl and mash with a fork.

Step 2:Mix with onion, red pepper, parsley, egg, and crumbs.

Step 3: Shape gently into four patties.

Step 4:Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and cook patties for about 5 minutes until browned and cooked through, turning once.

Recipe #3

  • Prep Time: 25 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
ounces ziti pasta 10
teaspoons olive oil 1
cup chopped onion 1/2

Step 1:Preheat oven to 375° F.

Step 2:Start the water for pasta.

Step 3:In a big pan sauté onion & garlic in oil over med-high heat for 2 min.

Step 4:Add meat, stirring to break it up. Cook for 3 min. until the pink is gone.

Step 5: Add sauce & pour 1/4 cup water into the jar, cover & shake, and pour the rest of the sauce into the pan. (meantime, start the pasta)

Step 6: Cook sauce for 10 minutes. Taste for salt & add extra spices if you like.

Step 7:Drain pasta, return to pasta cooking pot & stir in ricotta.

Step 8:Spread 1/2 cup sauce into a large baking pan (2 1/2 qt).

Step 9:Add 3 layers as follows: 1/3 of the ziti, 1/3 of the meat sauce, 1/3 of the mozarella, and 1/3 of the parmesan.

Step 10:Bake uncovered for 20 minutes.
